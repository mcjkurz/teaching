Softmax with Temperature Formula

$$P(\text{word}_i) = \frac{e^{\text{logit}_i / T}}{\sum_j e^{\text{logit}_j / T}}$$

Where T is the temperature parameter. Lower T makes the distribution sharper, higher T makes it more uniform.
